Soh Hong Guan's Fab Academy Learning Journey 2017

How to make almost anything

Ideal Motto of this class. Most people nowadays, will never think of building anything or repair anything own his own. With almost everything manufacture in China at affordable pricing, most people no longer tends make anything. Usually, their would just order directly from Taobao, Amazon, etc online and everything would be shipped to their home. Singapore Economy is at a junctual of transformation, capability and skillset to design and building of products and services has become a vital component of Skill Future. As one of the employee of Singapore first Polytechnic, we are also involve in this transformation to ensure what is being thought to our future students are relavent. I am glad to have embarked on Fab Academy 2017 which gives me the opportunity to relearn my skillsets and keep up to date with the technology and project management in order to be able to stay relevant. Come January 2017, let us go through my learning journey in trying to make almost anything.
